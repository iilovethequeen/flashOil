About Us

We are Flash Oil, an Oil Service company that has originated in Libya, taking its headquarters in its capital Tripoli, the company was founded in April-2024 with partnership contract with experienced Oil and Gas Sector Engineers, the company strives to provide high quality equipment and services to the local and International Companies, adhering to the highest industry standards.

Mission

To deliver high-quality energy resources that power industries and communities worldwide, while ensuring safety, efficiency, and environmental responsibility in everything we do.

Vision

To be a global leader in energy innovation — integrating traditional oil and gas excellence with renewable technologies to shape the energy systems of tomorrow.

Leadership & History

Founded with a passion for progress, Flash Oil has grown from a regional operator into a multinational energy group. Guided by a team of seasoned industry leaders, our company continues to pioneer advancements in exploration and production efficiency.
